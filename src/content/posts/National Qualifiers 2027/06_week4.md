> **WHY:** Supercompensation: the fitness adaptations from Weeks 1-3 are not fully realized until recovery allows structural remodeling. Tendons, mitochondria, and neuromuscular pathways consolidate during reduced load. This week should feel easy. That means it's working.

## Monday - Easy Run + Strides

- **Distance:** 5 km (shortest of the block)
- **Pace:** Z1-Z2 (5\:30-6\:00/km). Genuinely slow.
- **Strides:** 4 x 80m

*ABSORB the fitness from W1-3. Less is more.*

---

## Tuesday - Sharpening: Controlled Intervals

- **Pre-fuel:** 30-50g fast carbs 60 min before.
- **Warm-up:** 10 min easy jog into strides

**Workout:**

| Interval | Reps | Pace | Recovery |
|----------|------|------|----------|
| 600m | 4 | Z5 (3\:15-3\:30/km) | 3\:00 FULL recovery |

- **Total quality:** 2.4 km. Low volume, high quality.
- **Pacing:** Should feel **SMOOTH**. Not desperate. Full recovery between.
- **Breathing:** 2\:2 or 2\:1. Should feel more controlled than W1 at the same effort.
- **Cool-down:** 10 min easy jog

> **COACH'S NOTE:** FITNESS CHECK: If 3\:20/km feels controlled with stable breathing, you have made real progress from Day 1. Compare this session to your W1 Tuesday. The difference is your 4-week adaptation.
