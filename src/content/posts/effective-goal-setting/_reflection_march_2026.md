## Reflection on Internships, Goal Setting, Value, and Ego

The goal of writing this reflection is to identify what went wrong, what went well, and what to change in the upcoming internship cycle. Let's keep the scope small right now because we want to be percise, then extrapolate outwards.

### 2024-2025: My First Year in CS

Put simply, my internship search started in October of 2024. As I was trying to get back into CS in 2nd year, I applied inconsistently. Maybe around a week in October in which I was serious applying. Fast forward to March 2025, I realized I needed an internship in the summer so I began mass applying in the hopes I can remake it into the CS program and ALSO secure an internship. It turns out the exam pressure of the program was too high. Plus, march was late anyways. I'd spend January and February doing hackathons instead of applying and winter break was quite wasted.

Here are the key things I did poorly:
1) I underappreciated the importance of applying early (those march applications were basically useless)
2) I misallocated time after winning uofthacks in January to two more hackathons instead of applying to summer positions; put another way, I overcorrected for the importance of winning hackathons (when I could have applied in late jan - early feb)

1) Getting into CS was genuinely an important step, and I allocated time to that
