*March 24, 2026*

I hosted a Mozilla Engineering Talks + Networking event at the Mozilla Toronto office (366 Adelaide St W). The idea was simple: give students a chance to tour the workspace, meet Mozilla engineers and interns, and ask questions about careers in software, privacy, and open source.

The event ran from 11 AM to 1 PM. We had lunch, a few speaker presentations from Mozilla engineers, and open networking. People got to hear about what it's like working on Firefox, how AI is shaping the open web, and what privacy engineering actually looks like day to day.
