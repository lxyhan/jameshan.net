Notes on probability and statistical inference.

---

## Course Overview

An introduction to statistical inference and practice:

- Statistical models and parameters
- Estimators of parameters and their statistical properties
- Methods of estimation
- Confidence intervals
- Hypothesis testing
- Likelihood function
- The linear model

---

## The Articles

### Part I: Foundations

1. **Probability Review** — Random variables, distributions, expectation, variance
2. **Statistical Models** — Parameters, families of distributions, sufficient statistics

### Part II: Estimation

3. **Estimators & Properties** — Bias, variance, MSE, consistency, efficiency
4. **Method of Moments** — Matching sample and population moments
5. **Maximum Likelihood Estimation** — Likelihood function, MLE properties, Fisher information

### Part III: Inference

6. **Confidence Intervals** — Pivotal quantities, interval estimation, interpretation
7. **Hypothesis Testing** — Null/alternative, test statistics, p-values, power
8. **Likelihood Ratio Tests** — Neyman-Pearson, generalized LRT

### Part IV: Regression

9. **The Linear Model** — OLS, assumptions, Gauss-Markov theorem
10. **Inference for Linear Models** — t-tests, F-tests, prediction intervals
